A much-anticipated appearance by football icon Lionel Messi in Kolkata, India, as part of his 'GOAT Tour India 2025', descended into widespread chaos and fan vandalism on Saturday, December 13, 2025. The disorder at the Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan (Salt Lake Stadium) cast a severe shadow over what was meant to be a historic celebration of the sport.
